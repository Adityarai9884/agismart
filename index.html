Expert Advice

Seasonal Farming Tips

Practical tips from agronomists to help you maximise yield and reduce costs.

1

Soil Testing Before Sowing

Test your soil's pH and nutrient levels before every season to apply the right fertiliser and avoid overuse.

2

Use Certified Seeds

Always buy certified, disease-resistant seeds from authorised dealers to ensure better germination and higher yield.

3

Drip Irrigation Saves Water

Switch to drip or sprinkler irrigation to reduce water consumption by up to 50% while improving crop health.

4

Crop Rotation Benefits

Rotating legumes with cereals naturally enriches soil nitrogen, breaks pest cycles, and improves overall farm productivity.

5

Integrated Pest Management

Combine biological, cultural and chemical pest control methods to reduce pesticide costs and protect the environment.

6

Post-Harvest Storage

Store produce in cool, dry, ventilated storage. Use hermetic bags or warehouses to reduce post-harvest losses significantly.

Key Schemes for Farmers

Know your entitlements and benefits under central and state government programmes.

🌾 PM-KISAN Scheme

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi provides ₹6,000 per year to eligible farmer families in three equal instalments directly into their bank account.

Learn More →

🛡️ Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Crop insurance scheme covering losses from natural calamities, pests and diseases. Farmers pay minimal premium while government subsidises the rest.

Learn More →

🧪 Soil Health Card Scheme

Free soil health cards for all farmers providing information about soil nutrient status and fertiliser recommendations for 12 parameters.

Learn More →

💧 Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana

Ensures irrigation access at farm level, expanding cultivable area under assured irrigation and improving water use efficiency.
